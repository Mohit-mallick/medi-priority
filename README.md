## Making Healthcare Smarter, Faster, and More Accessible

Getting medical attention at the right time can be challenging, especially when healthcare systems are overloaded. Patients often face long waiting times, delayed appointments, and difficulty accessing the right specialist. At the same time, healthcare providers struggle with managing patient flow and prioritizing urgent cases effectively.

**MediPriority** is an AI-powered healthcare management platform built to address these challenges. It helps healthcare providers identify high-priority patients, automate routine processes, and improve the overall patient experience through intelligent decision-making.

By combining AI-powered patient assessment, urgency scoring, doctor matching, appointment scheduling, and post-consultation support, MediPriority creates a seamless healthcare journey from start to finish.

---

## 💡 Why MediPriority?

Traditional healthcare systems often follow a first-come-first-served approach, which may not always reflect the actual urgency of a patient's condition.

MediPriority introduces an intelligent prioritization system that evaluates patient information in real time and helps ensure that patients with the most urgent needs receive attention first.

### Our Goals

- Reduce waiting times
- Improve patient outcomes
- Support healthcare professionals
- Increase operational efficiency
- Deliver a better healthcare experience for everyone

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Patient Assessment
Patients can provide symptoms through text, images, or videos. AI models analyze the information and assist in identifying potential concerns before consultation.

### 📊 Dynamic Urgency Scoring
Patients are prioritized based on both medical urgency and real-world factors, ensuring fair and effective resource allocation.

### 🩺 Intelligent Doctor Matching
The platform recommends suitable healthcare professionals based on patient needs, specialties, and availability.

### 📅 Smart Appointment Scheduling
Appointments are scheduled dynamically, helping healthcare facilities optimize their resources while reducing delays.

### 🎙️ Real-Time Consultation Support
Speech recognition and AI assistance help capture important consultation details and improve communication.

### 💊 Medication & Follow-Up Assistance
Patients receive reminders, recommendations, and support after consultations to encourage better treatment adherence.

### 🧑‍⚕️ Personalized AI Health Assistant
An AI-powered virtual assistant helps patients manage their healthcare journey even outside the hospital environment.
